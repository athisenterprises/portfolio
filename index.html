What is a Game?

This essay allowed us to discuss the very foundamentals of what constitutes a game, how we define it, and we also studied the different methods of analyzing video games used by scholars in this field. Asking the very question of what constitutes a games allows us to better understand their importance in society as they are almost omnipresent in our lives

Violent Game Brochure

For this project, we've gathered and researched information about violence in video games and the culture of video games overall. I then created a brochure about a violent game: Manhunt, as I maintain a persuasive undertone with all elements inserted in the brochure.

Game History Timeline

This timeline showcases the history of Fifa. Both the business and technological aspects. We also positioned the recent introduction of Story Mode that challenges the definition we usually attribute to Fifa as a typical sports game. This timeline even puts in question the potential future for this game, as virtual reality is being experimented by several technology companies

Gamer Persona

For this project, I had the chance to bring to life my very own Haitan national hero: Jean Jacques Dessalines. I wrote an interesting piece that narrated how his story would have been portrayed if a video game was to be made about him. This was a very interesting and deep experience for me.
